What do we Actually Do

Every man, woman and child has the inalienable right to be free from hunger and malnutrition in order to develop fully and maintain their physical and mental faculties. So on this platform we try to help all the less fortunate ones who can't get their daily meals by distributing them atleast 2 meals a day. We do do this by mainly donations recieved by the public. We also connect with the Restraunts and Hotels to collect food remaining at the end of day from them and distributing it to the needy people. Our website also provides a platform to the NGOs who don't have a bigger reach. These small NGOs can register themselves on our website where people can see their previous works and also fund them. Apart from donating money, people can donate their time by volunteering with us in distributing the food.
